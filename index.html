ABOUT

Hi, I'm Micah Theo.

I'm a Connecticut-based singer-songwriter creating music inspired by real experiences, growing up, friendships, and the stories that stay with us long after they're over.

Whether performing live or recording in my home studio, my goal is simple: write honest songs that people can connect with.
